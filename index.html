Cozy Chai Latte

Ingredients:

  • 2 black tea bags
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cardamom pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1" fresh ginger
  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 2 tbsp honey

Instructions:

  1. Simmer spices in 1 cup water
  2. Add tea bags and steep
  3. Add milk and honey
  4. Strain and serve hot

Berry Mint Refresher

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 8-10 mint leaves
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Sparkling water
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Muddle berries and mint
  2. Add lime juice and honey
  3. Fill glass with ice
  4. Top with sparkling water
